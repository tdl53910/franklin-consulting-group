Franklin Consulting Group is a student-led consulting organization within the Franklin College of Arts and Sciences at the University of Georgia.

About Franklin Consulting Group

Franklin Consulting Group (FCG) was established through the Student Government Association to provide structured, client-based experiential learning and applied research opportunities for undergraduate students.

The organization centralizes access to real-world projects across the University of Georgia, connecting students with faculty, departments, and partners seeking interdisciplinary, solution-oriented work.

What We Do

  • Client-Based Projects

    Website development, student resource platforms, institutional tools, and technical support projects.

  • Applied Research

    Research assistance across the natural sciences, social sciences, humanities, and data-driven initiatives.

  • Interdisciplinary Cohorts

    Small, skill-aligned student teams working under faculty and organizational guidance.

For Students

Students selected for Franklin Consulting Group cohorts gain hands-on experience working on real projects, professional skill development, and exposure to interdisciplinary collaboration.

Applications are open to all Franklin College students and are evaluated based on skill alignment, interest, and project needs.

Faculty & Partners

Franklin Consulting Group collaborates with faculty members, university departments, and campus partners seeking undergraduate involvement in applied initiatives.

Partners may propose projects, advise student teams, or contribute to the development of the organization’s project pipeline.
